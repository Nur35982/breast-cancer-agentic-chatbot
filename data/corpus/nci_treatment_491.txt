In patients with a PD-L1 CPS of 10 or more, the median OS was 12.7 months (95% CI, 9.9–16.3) in the pembrolizumab group and 11.6 months (95% CI, 8.3–13.7) in the chemotherapy group (HR, 0.78; 95% CI, 0.57–1.06; log-rankP= .057).[63][Level of evidence A1] In patients with a CPS of 1 or more, the median OS was 10.7 months (95% CI, 9.3–12.5) in the pembrolizumab group and 10.2 months (95% CI, 7.9–12.6) in the chemotherapy group (HR, 0.86; 95% CI, 0.69–1.06; log-rankP= .073).
Among all patients, the median OS was 9.9 months (95% CI, 8.3–11.4) in the pembrolizumab group and 10.8 months (95% CI, 9.1–12.6) in the chemotherapy group (HR, 0.97; 95% CI, 0.82–1.15).
