Notably, the cumulative risk of endometrial cancer during years 5 to 14 from breast cancer diagnosis was 3.1% for women who received 10 years of tamoxifen versus 1.6% for women who received 5 years of tamoxifen.
The mortality for years 5 to 14 was 12.2 versus 15 for an absolute mortality reduction of 2.8%.The results of the ATLAS trial indicated that for women who remained premenopausal after 5 years of adjuvant tamoxifen, continued tamoxifen for 5 more years was beneficial.[43] Women who have become menopausal after 5 years of tamoxifen may also be treated with AIs.
